Getting Started

Welcome to the Daily Schedule App! This guide will help you get started with using the app effectively.

  • How to Add a New Schedule

    To add a new schedule, follow these steps:

    1. Navigate to the Daily Schedule page by clicking on the "Daily Schedule" link in the sidebar.
    2. Click on the "Add Schedule" button at the top of the page.
    3. Fill in the necessary details, such as the schedule name, image, and audio.
    4. Click the "Save" button to add the new schedule to your daily routine.

  • How to Edit Your Profile

    You can edit your profile information by following these steps:

    1. Click on the "Profile" link in the sidebar to navigate to your profile page.
    2. Click the "Edit Profile" button.
    3. In the modal that appears, update your profile details such as your name, age, and interests.
    4. Click the "Save" button to apply the changes.

FAQs

  • Q: How can I delete a schedule?

    A: To delete a schedule, follow these steps:

    1. Navigate to the Daily Schedule page by clicking on the "Daily Schedule" link in the sidebar.
    2. Find the schedule you want to delete and click on the "Delete" button next to it.
    3. Confirm the deletion in the prompt that appears.
    4. The schedule will be removed from your daily routine.

  • Q: How can I customize the sensory elements?

    A: To customize the sensory elements, follow these steps:

    1. Click on the "Settings" link in the sidebar to navigate to the settings page.
    2. Adjust the settings for sound effects, animations, and flashing lights based on your preferences.
    3. Your changes will be applied immediately.
